## 💡 Engineering Highlights for International Buyers

* **Advanced Ridge Formation:** The integrated ridging system creates optimal soil mounds for potato growth, improving drainage and tuber expansion.
* **Automatic Manuring:** High-accuracy manure tanks ensure fertilizer is placed precisely where the seed needs it, reducing waste and increasing yield.
