## 💡 Key Selling Points for International Buyers

### 1. Superior Pulverizing Performance
The 1BJX series is specifically designed for "intensive cultivation." It doesn't just turn the soil; it pulverizes clods, leaving a smooth, even surface that is ready for immediate planting.

### 2. Versatility in Linkage
While the standard **1BJX** caters to a wide power range (including large trailed versions for up to 6000mm width), the **1BJXS (New Type)** features a specialized linkage design that offers better weight distribution for medium-sized tractors, ensuring deeper penetration without extra ballast.

### 3. Logistic Optimization (LCL Friendly)
Middle-duty harrows can be expensive to ship. We provide optimized disassembling for container loading, allowing you to mix these harrows with tractors or other implements to lower your average freight cost.
