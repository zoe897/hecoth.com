# 1LYT Series One-way Disc Plough

<p class="lead-text">
  The 1LYT Series One-Way Disc Plough is an ideal solution for primary tillage on both cultivated and virgin land. It is specifically optimized for green manure fields with rice or wheat stubble, providing superior till-aging performance into the soil.
</p>

## 🌟 Key Performance Advantages

<div class="feature-grid">
  <div class="feature-card">
    <h3>🌱 Excellent Soil Inversion</h3>
    <p>Strong soil turnover and coverage capacity, effectively handling green manure and heavy stubble to satisfy agricultural production requirements.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <h3>⚙️ Low Working Resistance</h3>
    <p>Advanced structural design significantly reduces tilling resistance, leading to lower fuel consumption and less tractor strain.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="feature-card">
    <h3>🛠️ Easy Adjustment</h3>
    <p>User-friendly design makes it easy to operate and adjust, ensuring reliable performance across varying soil conditions.</p>
