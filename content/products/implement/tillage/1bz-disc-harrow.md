## 💡 Professional Insights for Importers

### 1. 1BZ vs. European Type: Which to choose?
While the **1BZ** is a cost-effective workhorse for standard heavy-duty tasks, the **European New Type (YJ-BZY/1BZ(G))** offers better maintenance access due to its combined structure and improved field leveling with wide flotation tires. For tractors above 200HP, the **1BZ(G)** is the recommended choice for maximum width and depth.

### 2. Disc Scraper & Bearing Quality
All our heavy harrows come with adjustable disc scrapers to prevent clogging in wet, sticky soil. We use high-performance sealed bearings to minimize downtime and extend maintenance intervals.

### 3. Logistical Advantage
Heavy harrows are bulky. We provide detailed disassembling guides to optimize container space (FCL). By consolidating these with tractors, you can significantly reduce your per-unit import cost.
