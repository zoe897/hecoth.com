# 1LYX Series Disc Plough

The **1LYX series disc plough** is completely mounted with a tractor. During operation, the disc rotates to turn the soil, making it particularly suitable for operation in fields with grasses, straw, and stems of crops or plants. It excels in soils with high resistance or those containing stones and broken brickbat.

### Key Advantages
* **Non-clogging:** Excellent performance in fields with heavy residue; the rotating discs cut through stems and weeds easily.
* **High Efficiency:** Durable construction with a focus on high-speed operation and stable soil turning.
* **Easy Adjustment:** Simple linkage and depth control to match various soil conditions.
* **Strong & Durable:** Built to withstand impact from underground obstacles like stones or bricks.
