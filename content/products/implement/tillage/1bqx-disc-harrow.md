## 💡 Buyer's FAQ & Solutions

### 1. Which configuration is better for my field?
The **1BQX (Offset)** is better for rougher fields and mixing soil with organic matter. The **1BQDX (Opposed)** offers more balanced stability and is better for creating a perfectly level seedbed after plowing.

### 2. Can I mix different implements in one container?
**Yes!** We specialize in supply chain integration. You can mix these light harrows with tractors, ploughs, or irrigation belts in a single **LCL or FCL container** to minimize your sea freight cost per unit.

### 3. Are spare parts available?
We provide **boron steel** or **65Mn spring steel** replacement discs. We recommend ordering an extra set of disc bearings and blades to ensure zero downtime during the busy season.
