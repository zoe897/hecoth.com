## Key Features & Advantages

- **Slit Outlet (Anti-Siphon):** The precision-engineered slit outlet opens only when under pressure. When water is turned off, the slit closes to prevent root intrusion and soil ingestion, making it ideal for sub-surface irrigation.
- **Continuous Labyrinth Flow Path:** Unlike discrete emitters, T-Tape features a long, turbulent flow path that keeps sediment in suspension, providing superior anti-clogging performance.
- **Exceptional Uniformity:** Delivers precise amounts of water and nutrients to every plant, even in extra-long lateral runs, ensuring uniform crop growth and maximum yields.
- **High Tensile Strength:** Built with high-quality Polyethylene (PE), T-Tape can withstand the mechanical stresses of high-speed installation without stretching or breaking.
