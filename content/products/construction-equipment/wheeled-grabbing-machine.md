## 💡 Engineering Highlights for Global Sourcing

* **Optimized Stability:** Features an extra-wide undercarriage and 4 reinforced hydraulic outriggers to ensure absolute stability when handling heavy loads at maximum extension.
* **Dual-Pump Flow Merging:** The advanced hydraulic system ensures smooth, combined movements of the boom and grapple, significantly reducing cycle times in high-volume environments.
* **Heavy-Duty Cooling System:** Designed with an oversized radiator and high-efficiency fan to handle the 24/7 continuous operation typical of recycling and port facilities.
* **Durability in Harsh Environments:** Critical hydraulic lines are protected with steel guards, and pivot points are reinforced to withstand the impacts of scrap and timber handling.
